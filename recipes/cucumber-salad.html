Cucumber Salad with Ginger

In this recipe I will describe how to prepare this easy and delicious asian style cucumber salad.

This easy to prepare cucumber salad is one of my favorites ever since I tried it the first time during my vacation at the beautiful Philippines. Here I'm going to share the way this dish is prepared so you can enjoy it too!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cucumber
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced onion
  • 1 tsp Ginger, finely minced
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Seeds

Steps

  1. Cut the cucumber into thin slices and add it to a bowl. Combine it with the sliced onion.
  2. Mix the ginger and soy sauce in a seperate bowl.
  3. Add the dressing to the prepared cucumber and toss until the cucumbers are fully coated in soy sauce.
  4. Add some sesame seeds and toss again.
  5. Serve the salad with a topping of sesmae seeds.
  6. Enjoy!
