Spicy Peanut Butter Noodles

In this recipe I will describe how to prepare these easy and delicious peanut butter noodles.

This dish is perfect for days on which you crave a hot meal but you don't want to cook for a long time. You can easily prepare this meal in as little as 15 minutes. Here I'll show you how to do this.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack instant noodles
  • 1-2 tbsp neutral plant oil
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter
  • 1 garlic glove
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds
  • 1 tsp sweetener (syrup, sugar, etc.
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Optionally any veggies you want to add.

Steps

  1. Heat up the plant oil in a pan.
  2. Mix garlic, chili flakes, ginger and sesame seeds in a bowl together.
  3. Pour the hot oil on top.
  4. Add peanut butter, sweetener and soy sauce and mix it all well together.
  5. Cook the instant noodles as described on the packaging.
  6. Add noodles to the sauce and mix well. Add water if needed.
  7. Add fired veggies you want to add.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
